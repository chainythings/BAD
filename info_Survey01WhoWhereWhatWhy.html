RESULTS OF B.A.D's RECENT SURVEY

Who, where, what and why #blockchainart, #cryptocollectibles and #nft?

 

Following up a discussion on the RAREAF2 telegram channels, we initiated a survey addressing blockchainart and cryptocollectibles artists, collectors, galerists, programers, asking them: Why are you active in the field? Which platforms do you use? How are you producing your artworks? How much profit do you make? And some more basics about NFT, education and value. We received xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx answers by May 11th, and are happy to present you the results right before #RAREAF2 will happen.

 

 

2019-05-11

Drone Graffiti


A combination of street art and blockchain - two of our absolute favorite topics? What a blast! People of the Drone Graffiti Project set themselves two goals: One is to fight ephemerality of urban artworks by storing them in a public distributed ledger. Second is to install collaborative artworks by help of drones and backed by blockchain technology on public spaces. Wow!
