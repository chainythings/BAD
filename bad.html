You reached the very core of B.A.D - The Blockchain Art Directory

Great you are here to have a look at what is going on in the #blockchainart and #cryptocollectibles field.

At the moment, you find all entries in form of a simple list, which comes from us not being very capable referring to coding. However, with the growth of contents, B.A.D will get features to sort and display submitted information.

You want to submit something as well? No problem!
