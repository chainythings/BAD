ONBOARDING: BLOCKCHAINART PLATFORMS & MARKET PLACES

Learn about the places where digital art is tokenized, sold, traded and exhibited - backed by blockchain technology

 

Arists who are new to the blockchain market are often confused about how their artworks become backed by blockchain technology, and where the tokenized pieces of art can be sold, traded and exhibited. Also, there are products available which can be used to certify and verify physical artworks as well as digital items. For collectors, it might be interesting to gain an overview of places where #blockchainart and #cryptocollectibles are sold.
For this reason, we compiled a list with platforms, marketplaces and all-inclusive dApps which can be used to and/or tokenize, buy and sell art on the blockchain.

* You are sure we missed a platform, market place or other important dApp relevant for this section? Then drop us a line via twitter or e-mail!

 

 

Tokenizing only

The first step to master when providing art backed by blockchain technology is to "put them on the blockchain", which is to "tokenize" the work. Platforms like the following ones provide the technology to tokenize art works, which then can be sold via third-party market places.

www.digitalartchain.com

Digital Art Chain

Blockchain: Ethereum
Standard: ERC-721

 

Gallery only

Your artwork is tokenized and you want to simply keep it and watch it online? Then, you can make use of a gallery dApp which allows you to exhibit it.

gallery.cryptogoods.com

CryptoGoods Gallery

Blockchain: Ethereum
Standard: ERC-721

 

Tokenizing and gallery

If you want to show your tokenized art works, and prepare them for being sold on a third-party market place, you can revert to the following solutions.

www.codex-viewer.com

Codex Viewer

Blockchain: Ethereum
Standard: ERC-20; ERC-721

 

Marketplace and gallery

Once you own an artwort which is tokenized, you can offer it on an independent market place. Of course, it is important to choose a market place which covers the type of blockchain, and within the standard which your token has.

www.opensea.io

OpenSea

Blockchain: Ethereum
Standard: ERC-20; ERC-721

www.opensea.io

Decentralised Mobile Browsers

You are mega busy and always in a rush? Never mind, decentralised mobile browsers might be the perfect solution for you. With mobile browsers, you can have one or more accounts, access the decentralised web without additional dApps or extensions, and some even have an integrated gallery feature which allows you to carry you rare digital tokens around with you all the time. Isn't this rare art collector's heaven?
