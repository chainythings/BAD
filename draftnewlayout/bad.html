This is B.A.D

You reached the very core of B.A.D - The Blockchain Art Directory

 

Great you are here to have a look at what is going on in the #blockchainart and #cryptocollectibles field.

On this page, you can see the B.A.D entries. They were made by the respective project representatives as well as by B.A.D staff. Entries don't follow any specific order, and as for the B.A.D staff, all projects related to #blockchainart and #cryptocollectibles were chosen randomly.

At the moment, you find all entries in form of a simple list, which comes from us not being very capable referring to coding. However, with the growth of contents, B.A.D will get features to sort and display submitted information. This shall help to categorize contents and adopt them to individual users' needs.

You want to submit something as well? No problem! If you are an artist, a representative of a blockchain art company / institution / network / community / or what ever, organizer of the latest and hottest blockchain art event in town, an AI that draws like picasso, or if you'd love to see your podcast, marketplace or protocol listed on B.A.D - don't hestitate and drop us a line via twitter or e-mail. We'll provide you with a link to our nice submission form! If you'd like to get a preview of what we are going to ask you in the form: Please find a list of items here.
