ONBOARDING: #BLOCKCHAINART PLATFORMS & MARKET PLACES

Places where digital art is tokenized, sold, traded and exhibited - backed by blockchain technology

 

Arists who are new to the blockchain market are often confused about how their artworks become backed by blockchain technology, and where the tokenized pieces of art can be sold, traded and exhibited. Also, there are products available which can be used to certify and verify physical artworks as well as digital items.
For collectors, it might be interesting to gain an overview of places where #blockchainart and #cryptocollectibles are sold.
For this reason, we compiled a list with platforms, marketplaces and all-inclusive dApps which can be used to and/or tokenize, buy and sell art on the blockchain.

 

 

Tokenizing only

The first step to master when providing art backed by blockchain technology is to "put them on the blockchain", which is to "tokenize" the work. Platforms like the following ones provide the technology to tokenize art works, which then can be sold via third-party market places.

www.digitalartchain.com

Digital Art Chain

Blockchain: Ethereum
Standard: ERC-721

 

Gallery only

Your artwork is tokenized and you want to simply keep it and watch it online? Then, you can make use of a gallery dApp which allows you to exhibit it (in some cases publicly).

gallery.cryptogoods.com

CryptoGoods Gallery

Blockchain: Ethereum
Standard: ERC-721

 

Marketplace only

Once you own an artwort which is tokenized, you can offer it on an independent market place. Of course it is important to choose a market place which covers the type of blockchain and within the standard which your token has.

www.opensea.io

OpenSea

Blockchain: Ethereum
Standard: ERC-20; ERC-721

www.opensea.io

Book of Orbs

Blockchain: Bitcoin

All-in-one Solutions

If you want a solution that covers it all - tokenization, marketplace and gallery - you can become member of one of the following platforms (most are working on "invitation only" and "apply" basis).
